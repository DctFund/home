Start Dreaming

We live in an age where the real and virtual worlds intersect, and a ceaseless flood of new things keep pushing progress, until real life and the virtual world start to blur together. In the past, ordinary people working in the real world could only realize their dreams by laboring for them. They couldn’t imagine using technology to change their own lives and make their dreams a reality. But today, data puts everything in reach.

No one can deny that behind technology lie what were once just dreams. Really, it isn’t technology that changes the world, but aspiration. Therefore, this group of ordinary people have established the Dreams Come True Foundation, and they hope to help even more “dreamers” who are in need to realize their hopes—because any little dream could be a force to change the world.

The Dreams Come True Foundation’s deepest wish is to provide a place for dreams, to make charity more transparent and easier, and to help innovation find more places to work. All of that is based on a hope to make the world better.

The Dreams Come True Foundation subdivides all donations to four purposes: 35% is donated to other charitable foundations; 35% goes towards innovative technology teams; 15% is used for additional fundraising; and 15% goes towards operational costs.

In the future, the Dreams Come True Foundation hopes to lead still more organizations to push for progress, and to build a more beautiful world together.
